Privacy Policy

Met is designed to store only the information you intentionally add or import. We do not want to become an inbox reader or a surveillance layer on top of your relationships.

Information you intentionally provide

Met may store contact details, context notes, tags, and saved drafts that you create inside the app. If you import a contact from Apple Contacts, we only import the fields needed to create a Met record.

What Met does not do by default

Met does not scan your email inbox, scrape message history, or silently import your full address book. The product is intentionally designed around user-directed capture and explicit imports.

Third-party integrations

Optional integrations may request access to specific device features or providers. If Met later offers calendar or contacts enhancements, those permissions will remain opt-in and limited to the feature you are using.
